## 📄 **Customer Support Document – Page 1: Account & Login**

### **1. How do I create an account?**

To create an account:

* Visit our website and click on **"Sign Up"**.
* Fill in the required information (name, email, password).
* Check your email for a verification link and follow it.

### **2. I forgot my password. How can I reset it?**

* Click on the **"Forgot Password"** link on the login page.
* Enter your registered email address.
* Check your email for a reset link and follow the instructions.

### **3. Can I change my email address?**

Yes, go to:

* **Settings > Account > Change Email**
* Enter your new email and verify it through the confirmation email we send.

### **4. How do I delete my account?**

To delete your account:

* Go to **Settings > Account > Delete Account**
* Confirm your password and reason for leaving.
* Note: This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

### **5. Why am I locked out of my account?**

This could be due to:

* Too many failed login attempts.
* Suspicious activity.
* Reach out to [support@yourcompany.com](mailto:support@yourcompany.com) to verify your identity and unlock your account.

## 📄 **Customer Support Document – Page 2: Billing & Payments**

### **1. What payment methods do you accept?**

We accept:

* Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
* PayPal
* Bank Transfers (for annual subscriptions)
* Mobile wallets (in supported regions)

### **2. Where can I find my invoices?**

* Log in and go to **Settings > Billing > Invoices**.
* You can download each invoice in PDF format.

### **3. How do I update my payment method?**

* Navigate to **Settings > Billing > Payment Method**.
* Click “Edit” next to your current method.
* Enter your new card or payment details and save.

### **4. I was charged twice. What should I do?**

We apologize for the inconvenience.

* Please email **[billing@yourcompany.com](mailto:billing@yourcompany.com)** with both transaction IDs.
* Our team will investigate and issue a refund if applicable within 3–5 business days.

### **5. Can I switch between monthly and annual plans?**

Yes:

* Go to **Settings > Subscription**
* Choose between monthly or annual.
* Any unused balance from your current plan will be credited accordingly.
